  1. Cherry Blossom Noodle Cafe

    One of the best noodle shops in the Phoenix valley. They have great variations of noodle bowls including seafood, vegetable, traditional, udon, etc.

    Location: Phoenix, AZ
    Reservation Required?: No, but accepted
    Parking: Small; limited

  2. Manna BBQ

    Great for families and friends who want to enjoy Korean BBQ for an all-you-can eat restaurant experience.

    Location: Glendale, AZ
    Reservation Required?: No, but accepted
    Parking: Large; may get busy quickly

  3. Nishikawa Ramen

    Another ramen place with a great respect to traditional Japanese ramen style quisine. It may not be as good as the real Japan, but a great experience regardless.

    Location: Scottsdale, AZ
    Reservation Required?: No, but accepted
    Parking: Small; may get busy quickly

  4. Kura Sushi

    Fun, enjoyable experience for those who wish to try a revolving sushi restaurant. Automated drink server and ability to order your own specialty dish as an option.

    Location: Chandler, AZ
    Reservation Required?: No
    Parking: Large;

  5. Cafe Rio

    Fast-food chain but with great mexican food options in a salad bowl or wrap version. Very filling for a $10 bowl and anything leftover can be saved for another meal.

    Location: Valley-Wide, AZ
    Reservation Required?: No
    Parking: Medium; may get busy quickly
